Talent Acquisition and Recruitment Program through CFD Training (Online Mode) Last date of Registration: 7th Jan 2024

Eligibility Criteria: - Bachelors (3rd year/4th year, completed degree) and/or Masters (1st year/2nd year, completed Master’s degree) in Aerospace, Automobile and Mechanical Engineering Minimum qualifications: 75% or 8.0 CGPA in B.Tech. (Aerospace, Automobile, Mechanical) /M.Tech. (Aerospace, Automobile, Mechanical). Those who are pursuing B.Tech. or M.Tech. can calculate marks up to last semester. 75% in PCM (10th and 12th).

Further Process :-

Selection process will be started after training. After training examination will be conducted, those who will pass this exam will automatically advance in the talent-acquisition process of the project work by the institute. This selection will be based on interview performance (Online mode). The successful candidates in interview will be recruited for the post of “Assistant Engineer (B) “ The responsibility and working will be discussed through training. Working in online mode is also possible. Age limit: 28 years (As on 31st Dec. 2023) Salary: 20,000/pm to 36,000/pm

Agenda of the training : -

This training is a part of hiring process. This training will focus on the solution of the specific project. How to simulate accurately using RANS model for industrial applications. Understanding the turbulence modelling How to develop the mathematical model for fluid and heat transfer phenomena. Training will start on 25th Jan 2024. Duration : 4 weeks Training Fee: INR 12000/- (Twelve Thousand Only)
